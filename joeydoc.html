joey bada$$ & the pro era

tour documentary

On assignment for FADER TV, I boarded the Pro Era tour bus as a one-woman crew in the final days of their 2013 tour to document the crew's return home to New York City. After a week of fliming from each morning's breakfast to the aftermath of each night's show, I edited this 16-minute tour doc that was published on The FADER on May 23, 2011.
