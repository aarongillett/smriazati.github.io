coal: a love story

interactive film

An interactive exploration of America's complicated love affair with coal, designed to illumintate the viewer's personal connection to coal.

This project was the 2011 edition of The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill's Powering a Nation, a fellowship / project that gives students the opportunity to experiment with journalism.

As one of ten fellows, my roles included lead web designer, front-end programmer and motion graphic editor. In the trailer embedded above, I designed, animated and edited the piece.
