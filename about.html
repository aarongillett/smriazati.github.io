Sarah Riazati is a freelance storyteller with experience in video production, animation, photography and interactive design.

From 2011 to 2014, she was a video and interactive producer at Cornerstone Agency and The FADER Magazine. Before that she was a student at UNC-Chapel Hill's School of Journalism and Mass Communication, specializing in visual communication.

She is based in Brooklyn and available for travel.

This portfolio site is built from scratch with some help from around the internet, Oswald, Font Awesome and GitHub.
