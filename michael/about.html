France has recently enacted the new immigration law which strictly rules over migration flows. The new law makes papers hard to be obtained, it introduces the integration contract, it makes detention before expulsion longer (from 12 to 32 days) and it introduces fingrprinting to all the ones asking for visas or residence papers. Migrants become "working tools" to labour market, but they are invisible people who hold no rights.
